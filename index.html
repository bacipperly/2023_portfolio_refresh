Throughout my career, I have been dedicated to advocating for the user and finding ways to bridge the gap between their needs and the design of a system. I have led various design processes, including workshops, co-design sessions, and user testing, and have worked with a variety of clients such as the NFL, Toyota, Fender Guitar, LinkedIn, and Dickies.

I find great satisfaction in working with my colleagues to create a collaborative environment that values trust and respect, ensuring that the user's voice is reflected in the final design.

I am always looking to learn new things and improve my skills to stay current in the field.
